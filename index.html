Only works if the Jagex launcher has been terminated via the taskbar

  • Switch to English
        jagex:launch=runescape,args=[configURI=rs-launch://www.runescape.com/k=5/l=0/jav_config.ws]

  • Switch to German
        jagex:launch=runescape,args=[configURI=rs-launch://www.runescape.com/k=5/l=1/jav_config.ws]

  • Switch to France
        jagex:launch=runescape,args=[configURI=rs-launch://www.runescape.com/k=5/l=2/jav_config.ws]

  • Switch to PT
        jagex:launch=runescape,args=[configURI=rs-launch://www.runescape.com/k=5/l=3/jav_config.ws]

  • Switch to an active Beta
        jagex:launch=runescape,args=[configURI=rs-launch://www.runescape.com/jav_config_beta.ws] (Jagex Launcher)
